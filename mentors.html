About

The Decentralised Autonomous Education System (DAES) empowers learners with a platform to submit Bitcoin project ideas for funding and mentorship. Leveraging DAES' decentralised nature, we foster an inclusive environment for innovative ideas to flourish. Weekly interactive lessons fueled by community efforts enable skill sharing and staying informed amidst Bitcoin's rapid developments.
